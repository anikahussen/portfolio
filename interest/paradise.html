LOGIC

Under Pressure one of the first albums I heard from logic and ever since then, He doesn't stick to one genre, which is pretty rare to see in the music industry. His music, like many hip hop artists has cinematic influences. As a student at the intersection of film and another subject, I appreciate that about this artists. To me he is very inspiring in that he worked hard to get out of the tough situations he had to deal with. The The Under Pressure album includes:

  • Buried Alive
  • Under Pressure
  • Gang Related

Everybody is what people know the most because he broke out to the mainstream crowd with his 1800-273-8255 song, advocating for mental health. This album coming out during a politically trifling time had a specific impact with Logic's openness to talk directly about issues. The EveryBody Album includes:

  • Killing Spree
  • America
  • Anziety

Bobby Tarantino II is one of Logic's more recent works. In fact, it came after the Everybody debut and all his songs on the album hit the Top trending list on Spotify the first week. (The album even has a Rick & Morty feature.) Bobby Tarantino II includes:

  • Boom Trap Protocol
  • Contra
  • Yuck

Young Sinatra is the newest of Logics full works. Young Sinatra is one of Logic's alter ego, characterized as classy and matter a fact. It will be his last Young Sinatra work, which is bittersweet because Young Sinatra was one of the first things I hear from him. (His most recent fastest verse is on the album) Young Sinatra IV includes:

  • 100 Miles and Running
  • Wu Tang Foreever
  • ICONIC

This is one of my favorite albums because logic infuses science-fiction and storytelling into his music. If you look carefully at the album covers, you can thread stories together. Currently, in The Incredible True Story, Logic and the gang are trying to find sustainable life (dream) after the destruction of the world. This is where the great and infamous Paradise comes from. The Incredible True Story includes:

  • Fade Away
  • City of Starsr
  • Stainless

Young Sinatra Undeniable was an earlier Young Sinatra mixtapes and it is an iconic blend of nostalgia, prime and masterfully cinematic. The Young Sinatra Undeniable mixtape includes:

  • Inception
  • Dead Presidents III
  • What You Want
